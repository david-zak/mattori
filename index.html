About Mattori

Our company mission is to be recognised as a business that brings values to the property industry. For us that means adhering to values of honesty, support and trust, all done in a professional and approachable manner. We both joined forces to offer something different, a true meaning to the word that is used so often yet with no substance… “personal”.

Your own personal contact who is there throughout, whenever you need them, whatever the time or day. Think of the benefit of having a friend in the property industry… one you can ask those questions at 10 o clock at night, just because it’s on your mind… well that’s how we work and those are the relationships we strive to build. We aren’t just about getting your property on our books… we are about building fantastic relationships that last.

Our approach

We ensure that we generate the enquiries that in most cases lead to the 1st viewing, you can’t beat getting to know your buyers and being able to introduce them to your property.

Why choose us?

We have worked in the industry for over 30 years combined and more recently together at one of the most recognised and exclusive independent estate agents in Europe.

Our guarantee

We will co-ordinate the process with you and all the other parties that need to be involved. Implementation of the chosen solution will be quick and stress free.
